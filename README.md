## 🌫 Purpose & Philosophy

Aether supports anyone who:
- Feels overwhelmed by emotions or information
- Needs a gentle push to start self-reflection
- Wants a calm, structured way to practice mindfulness and personal growth

> *Calm does not mean empty. Soft does not mean weak.*

## 🧠 How It Works

1. Navigate Zones: Explore Grounding, Regulation, Reflection
2. Engage with Cards: Expand each block to see exercises or prompts
3. Practice & Reflect: Go at your own pace
4. Experience Gentle Mastery: Subtle interactions guide focus and attention
